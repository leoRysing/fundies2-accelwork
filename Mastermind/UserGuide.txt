HOW TO PLAY:

Your goal is to guess the secret combination of colors 

1. Click on the colored circles to select which colors to input
2. Press the enter key when all black cricles are filled 
3. Read the text prompt for hints about how close your guess is to the correct answer
4. Colors that are nearby are the correct colors but in the wrong spot, colors that are correct are the right color in the right place
5. Press the backspace key to delete your current guess in case you make a mistake
6. When your guesses run out without a correct answer, you lose; You win with a fully correct guess!
